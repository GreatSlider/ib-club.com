Technology of mastering foreign languages

This course will deal with all the key aspects of learning a foreign language: how to properly learn the grammar of foreign languages, vocabulary, reading, writing, listening, pronunciation, speaking.

During the course you:

  • Learn the techniques of memorizing foreign words and phrases with which you can memorize dozens of foreign words a day with translation into long-term memory;
  • Learn to memorize grammar without "cramming" and automatically apply it in colloquial speech;
  • You will work out the methods of setting the correct pronunciation, as well as the perception of speech by ear;
  • You will learn how to effectively remember hieroglyphics;
  • Learn to memorize irregular and phrasal verbs, articles, idioms;
  • Understand the technique of memorizing dialogs and texts;
  • Remove the language barrier of the language.

At the end of the course you will have a clear scheme for learning a foreign language. Thanks to which you can learn any foreign language in less than 1 year.
