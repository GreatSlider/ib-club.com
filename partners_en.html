QUALIFICATION:
AGENT

Only registered and cryptoportfolio partners participate in the affiliate program. The basis of the affiliate program is a linear marketing plan. All 10 levels are open for the qualification of Agent.

QUALIFICATION:
MANAGER

The qualification of Manager is closed when the accumulated investment turnover in the structure is $25,000. Upon reaching the qualification of Manager a one-time premium of $1000 is charged (in Bitcoin at the exchange rate).

QUALIFICATION: GENERAL MANAGER

The qualification of General Manager is closed when the accumulated investment turnover in the structure is $50,000. Upon reaching the qualification of General Manager a one-time premium of $ 2000 is charged (in Bitcoin at the exchange rate).

QUALIFICATION: LEADER

The qualification of Leader is closed when the accumulated investment turnover in the structure is $ 100,000 and when there are two leaders in the structure of General Manager qualification in different branches. Upon reaching the qualification of Leader a one-time premium of $5000 is charged (in Bitcoin at the exchange rate).

QUALIFICATION:
DIRECTOR

The qualification of Director is closed when the accumulated investment turnover is $5,000,000 and when there are two leaders in the structure of Chief Specialist qualification in different branches. Upon reaching the qualification of Director a one-time premium of $75,000 is charged (in Bitcoin at the exchange rate) and a premium car is presented.

QUALIFICATION: MANAGING DIRECTOR

The qualification of Managing Director is closed when the accumulated investment turnover in the structure is $10,000,000 and when there are two leaders in the structure of Director qualification in different branches. This rank allows you to have income from the work of the structure on the first 10 levels.
