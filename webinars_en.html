Teaching Webinars

Professional specialists of the IBC Club regularly conduct training events
in the form of free webinars with free access for all comers.

The task of such events is the dissemination of information about the products and services of the Club.
Free training webinars are held in a convenient (for most potential listeners) time,
which allows a wide range of Club members with all the opportunities that are available in our Club.
For Club members, training webinars are an excellent opportunity to invite future Club members
to get acquainted with useful information without any effort on your part.
